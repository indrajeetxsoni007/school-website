Founded in 2017, S.T.S inter college has been a beacon of quality education in Ral for over 10 years. What started as a small primary school has grown into a full-fledged K–12 institution, shaping thousands of young minds.

Affiliated to CBSE, we follow a curriculum that balances national standards with global perspectives. Our sprawling 10-acre campus houses state-of-the-art facilities, and our dedicated faculty brings passion and expertise to every classroom.

🌟
Our Mission
To inspire every student to achieve their full potential through innovative education, strong values, and a love of learning.
🔭
Our Vision
To be the most respected school in the region by developing globally competent, empathetic, and responsible citizens.
💎
Our Values
Integrity, Curiosity, Inclusivity, Innovation, and Excellence — these principles guide every decision we make.

Admissions 2025–26

We welcome applications for all grades from Nursery to Grade 11. Our admission process is transparent, fair, and focused on understanding each child's potential.

1
Fill the Enquiry Form
Complete the online enquiry form with basic details of the student and family.
2
Document Submission
Submit birth certificate, previous marksheets, Aadhar card, and 4 passport photographs.
3
Interaction Session
A friendly interaction with the student and parents by our Academic Counselor.
4
Fee Payment & Enrollment
Pay the admission fee online or at the accounts office to confirm your seat.
